3.1.0 for 6.0.1
Released ???

   * Added /spawndex, which spawns Pokémon from their Pokédex number. Mostly useful for testing.
   * Fixed missing spaces in front of the pipes when showing EVs in /checkstats.
   * Made the /checkstats messages referring to /checkegg a bit more... professional.
   * Fixed a bunch of debug logger typos and wrong mentions.
   * Redid the internal config loader. Config errors now tell you more, and the mod is much smaller.
   * Changing command aliases no longer requires a reboot.
   * Removed per-config settings, as they were useless. Your old configs will still work.
   * Reworded several helper messages on configs to make more sense.
   * Fixed /pixelupgrade (the command list) throwing an error when used from console.
   * Added all babies and baby-based evolutions to /upgradeivs, and edited messages to fit.
   * Added Poipole (ID 803) and up to /checktypes and the new /spawndex.
